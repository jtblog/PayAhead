What is PayAhead?

PayAhead is a product designed to solve the problem of long queues in shopping malls, at filling stations etc thereby drive transparency, accuracy and accountability while taking away the bottleneck around reconciliation. 

Who is the product designed for?

The product is actually designed for those with retail stores, filling stations, shopping malls etc.

FAQ

How do you install PayAhead ?

On PC -

Visit payahead.com using Chrome (Latest). 

Click on the menu button at the top right corner (represented as three dots)

Click on install PayAhead then click install from the pop up

Alternatively, you can just click on the plus button in the address bar

On Android -

Visit payahead.com using Chrome mobile

Click on the menu button at the top right corner (represented as three dots)

Click on Add to Home screen. Then click on Add

On iPhone -

Visit payahead.com using Safari browser

Tap the share button. Tap the icon labeled Add to Home Screen

Tap add in the upper right corner. Name as PayAhead and tap add

How do I pay for goods and services ?

Only registered users can pay with PayAhead

Login in to PayAhead.

Click on the Users tab.

Type search words into the box then click on search or click on QR and place your camera on a QR code to search for user.

Click on desired vendor and click on pay. Next enter amount and click on proceed to checkout.

On checkout, select any method of payment that is convenient for you.

Does PayAhead save my card details ?

No. PayAhead works with a PCIDSS compliant payment processor for the security of your data.

Must I always enter a safetoken/OTP ?

Yes

What is the meaning of safetoken/OTP ?

One-Time Password or OTP is used to authenticate a card-not-present transaction (Master or Verve card)
